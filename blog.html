Interesting Facts

Gojo has a sweet tooth and is often seen enjoying various sweets and desserts. This playful side of him contrasts with his serious demeanor in battles. Despite his immense power, Gojo often acts silly and carefree. He loves to tease his friends and students, and his playful antics provide comic relief in the series. Gojo’s popularity isn’t just limited to the story. In real life, he has a massive fan following. He consistently ranks high in popularity polls and is often a subject of memes and fan discussions. Gojo’s distinct look, with his white hair and blindfold or sunglasses, makes him a popular choice for cosplay at anime conventions. His character is instantly recognizable and loved by many.
